COUGH IN NEW BORN

Introduction

Coughing is our body’s reaction to protect itself from allergens.

Most of the times cough happen due to viral infection.

Usually, it does not need medication if fever is not associated. 

Remedies

Steam Inhalation

Steam inhalation helps to remove excessive phlegm.

It also soothes the respiratory tract.

How to use it?

Switch on the hot shower for a while.

It will turn the bathroom like a steam room.

Sit there with your child for 10 minutes.

It will help to release the chest congestion.

Basil

Basil is an amazing herb that boosts immunity.

It also promotes respiratory health.

How to use it?

Make a paste of basil leaf (1-2 leaves), ginger (small piece), and honey (1/4 tsp).

your child lick this mixture 1-2 times a day for continuous 3 days.

Due to these agents, it's good to keep up electrolyte imbalance.

Ginger

Ginger is a very good anti-inflammatory.

Dut to this, it is helpful in cough present along with expectorant.

How to use it?

Take grated ginger (small piece) and honey (1/4 tsp).

Mix them well.

Symptoms

  • Runny nose
  • Slight fever
  • A dry or wet cough

Cause

  • Dry cough - cold/allergy
  • Wet cough - a bacterial infection.
