HYPERPIGMENTATION

Introduction

Hyperpigmentation is dark, patchy spots on the skin.

It happens due to the overproduction of melanin.

Melanin is a pigment.

It is responsible for the color of skin, hair, and eyes.

Remedies

Turmeric

Turmeric is one of the best herbs.

It helps to flush out the toxins from the body.

Its external use keeps the skin clear.

How to use it?

Take ½ teaspoon of turmeric.

Add one tablespoon of milk to it.

Apply this pack all over the face twice a day.

For a good result, repeat this every day for a minimum of two months.

Almond oil & kumkuma (saffron) face pack

Almonds are rich in Vitamin E.

Vitamin E reduces discoloration and helps to keep skin glowing.

Kumkuma is saffron and keeps skin pigmentation free.

How to use it?

Take one teaspoon of almond oil.

Add one or two strands of saffron to it.

Mix them well.

Massage your face with this oil at night before sleep.

Symptoms

  • Dark spots on the face.

Cause

  • Stress
  • Sun exposure
  • Improper nutrition

Do's

Stay hydrated every day.

Scrub your face twice a week.

Apply sunscreen always before going out in the sun.

Dont's

Avoid stress.

Avoid a sauna bath.

Avoid retinoid-based creams.
