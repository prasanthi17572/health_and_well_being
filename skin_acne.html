ACNE

Introduction

Sebaceous glands produce a variety of fat (sebum). This fat creates an environment to form acne.

Acne occurs due to clogged sebum and dead skin cells under the skin.

It commonly occurs in teenagers. But, it can happen at any age. 

Remedies

Aloe vera gel

Aloe vera gel has fantastic benefits on all types of skin problems.

How to use it?

Apply aloe vera gel on your face everyday twice a day.

Neem

Neem is an excellent antibacterial.

Due to this, it clears bacteria and keeps skin healthy.

How to use it?

Take a 1/2 teaspoon of neem powder and mix with 1/2 teaspoon of honey.

Apply this neem face pack on your face.

Keep it for 15 minutes.

Wash it off with cold water.

Repeat this three times a week.
