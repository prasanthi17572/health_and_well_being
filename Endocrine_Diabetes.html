DIABETES

Introduction

Diabetes is a metabolic disorder. It happens when the pancreas does not produce enough insulin Another cause is when the body does not respond to insulin appropriately.

The pancreas is an organ that produces insulin.

Insulin helps to use glucose obtained from food (carbohydrates) for energy. It is of three main types Type 1, Type 2, and gestational diabetes. 

Remedies

Bitter gourd

Bitter gourd has charantin, vicine, and insulin-like substances.

These substances have anti-diabetic properties.

They either work together or alone to regulate blood sugar levels.

How to use it?

Take one bitter gourd. Extract juice from it. Add some amount of water to it. Drink it empty stomach every morning.

Cinnamon

Cinnamon acts like insulin.

It does not let sugar levels increase in the blood.

It makes insulin more efficient in transmitting glucose to the cells.

Symptoms

  • Increased thirst
  • Frequent urination
  • Weakness
