CONSTIPATION

Introduction

Constipation is a condition of passing infrequent hard stools.

Remedies

Walk after meal

Walking after meals increases metabolism.

It gives relief from constipation.

How to do it?

Walk for a minimum of 15 minutes after each meal.

Stay Hydrated

Lack of water in the large intestine leads to the absorption of water from the stool.

It gives rise to constipation.

Drinking a sufficient amount of water keeps the body hydrated.

This way, intestines do not absorb excess water from the stool.

How much to drink?

Drink when you feel thirsty.

Start your day with two glasses of lukewarm water.

Drink least 2 to 3 liters per day according to your body's need.

Symptoms

  • Hard stool
  • Heaviness in stomach
  • Irregular passing of stool 

Cause

  • Low fiber diet
  • Improper nutrition
  • Less intake of water

Do's

Drink warm water only.

Eat dinner three hours before sleeping.

Don’t eat dinner after 7 pm.

Dont's

Avoid tea or coffee.

Don’t sleep after eating food.

Avoid eating refined items like refined sugar and flour.
