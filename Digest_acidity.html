ACIDITY

Introduction

Acidity is a regurgitation of the undigested food particles.

Sometimes it comes along with burning sensation in the chest.

Ayurveda suggests it may happen due to the imbalance of pitta (heat energy of the body). 

Remedies

Buttermilk with hing (Asafoetida)

Buttermilk is a simple remedy for acidity.

How to use it?

Drink one or two glasses a day for one week.

Add a pinch of asafoetida (hing) to it.

 

Coconut Water

Coconut water is hydrating and cooling in nature.

It helps to balance the pitta (heat energy of the body).

How to use it?

Drink a minimum of two glasses of coconut water every day for a week.

Symptoms

  • Heartburn
  • Belching
  • Vomiting

Cause

  • Stress
  • Indigestion
  • Eating spicy food

Do's

Eat evening meal before sunset.

Eat every meal at the same time of day.

Dont's

Don’t skip any meal.

Avoid tea and coffee.

Avoid heavy, spicy, and deep-fried food.
