Thyroid

Introduction

Increased formation of thyroxine hormone leads to hyperthyroidism. The thyroid gland produces thyroxine hormone.

This hormone is responsible for metabolism. 

Remedies

gooseberry(amla)

Indian gooseberry is very good for immunity.

It controls the thyroxine hormone production.

How to use it?

Drink amla juice twice a day. Follow this for a minimum of three months. 

Ashwagandha

Ashwagandha is also known as “Withania somnifera.” It is light in quality and hot in potency. In hyperthyroidism it helps to rejuvenate and strengthens the system.
