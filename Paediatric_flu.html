PREVENT FLU

Introduction

During the seasonal change, the ones with weak immunity suffer from cold and flu very easily.

Most common victims of flu are kids.

They are susceptible in a developing a cough, cold and flu quickly.

But, if we take proper care flu can be prevented naturally.

 

Remedies

Honey

During the seasonal change, make your baby lick honey and basil mixture twice a day.

This will help to reduce increased kapha and improve the immunity of a child.

 

Massage

Give your child a gentle massage with warm oil every day.

Perform massage 15 minutes before bath.

Massaging every day strengthens the joints and supports general health.

Using sesame oil for everyday purpose.

 

Honey & Lemon

In case, your baby is suffering chest congestion.

Apply below said:

Before bathing, apply honey and lemon on the baby’s chest.

This is the best cure for kids during seasonal change.
