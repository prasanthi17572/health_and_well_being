VOMITING

Introduction

Vomiting is throwing out of undigested food particles.

It may cause dehydration if not taken care of properly. 

Remedies

Cinnamon

Cinnamon helps to reduce stomach irritation.

How to use it?

Take a pinch of cinnamon powder.

Boil it with one glass of water.

Drink it once a day.

 

Rice Water

Vomiting may lead to dehydration due to water loss.

Drinking rice water hydrates the body well.

It helps to keep the sufferer away from dehydration.

How to use it?

Take five tablespoons of rice.

Boil it in two cups of water for ten minutes.

Retain its water and drink it.

Symptoms

  • Dehydration
  • Stomach pain
  • Throwing out from the mouth

Cause

  • Food poisoning
  • Travel sickness 

Do's

Take a rest.

Keep yourself hydrated.

Eat light food like khichdi or rice water only.

Dont's

Avoid dairy products.

Stay away from stinky things or places.
