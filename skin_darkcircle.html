DARK CIRCLES

Introduction

The dark circle is darkening of the skin under the eye region.

Sometimes, it looks embarrassing as it makes you look older.

Remedies

Sunscreen

Sunscreen is beneficial to protect skin from harmful UV rays.

It does not let melanin to produce in excess under eyes area.

This way, it decreases the dark circles.

How to use it?

Use sunscreen with a minimum of 30 SPF.

Apply it all over the face 15 minutes before going out.

Do not massage your face with it.

Let it get absorbed by the skin.

Caffeine

When the cause behind dark circles is aging, then using a coffee pack can be beneficial.

Caffeine in coffee maintains collagen present under the skin.

It diminishes dark circles caused by aging.

How to use it?

Clean your face properly with organic face wash.

Mix one teaspoon of coffee with water.

Make a paste.

Apply it on the face.

Leave it for 15 minutes under the eyes.

Wash it with the cold water.
